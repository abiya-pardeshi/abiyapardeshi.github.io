## 📊 1. Marketing Funnel & Revenue Analysis

### 🔍 Problem
Lack of visibility into how marketing contributes to pipeline and revenue.

### ⚙️ Approach
- Built full funnel: Lead → MQL → SQL → Customer  
- Wrote SQL queries to calculate:
  - Win Rate  
  - Revenue per Lead  
  - Conversion % across stages  

### 📈 Key Insights
- Significant drop-off at **MQL → SQL stage**  
- Low overall conversion rate from leads to customers  
- Certain business units driving disproportionate revenue  

### 💡 Business Impact
- Identified opportunity to improve conversion by **20–30%**  
- Enabled better allocation of marketing budget  

### 🧠 What This Shows
Funnel thinking + SQL + ability to connect data with revenue impact

## 📊 2. GA4 Bot Traffic Analysis & Data Accuracy

### 🔍 Problem
Inflated website metrics due to bot traffic, leading to misleading insights.

### ⚙️ Approach
- Analyzed engagement rate, session duration, and traffic patterns  
- Identified bot traffic using:
  - 0% engagement rate  
  - abnormal spikes  
- Evaluated filtering strategies (GA4 + GTM level)

### 📈 Key Insights
- Large % of sessions were non-human  
- Core metrics like traffic and conversions were unreliable  

### 💡 Business Impact
- Improved data trust and reporting accuracy  
- Enabled better campaign decision-making  

### 🧠 What This Shows
Strong analytical thinking + real-world data problem solving
